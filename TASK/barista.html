Our Menu

Espresso

An espresso drink starts with a small, strong shot of coffee made by forcing hot water through finely ground beans. It’s the base for many coffee drinks, like lattes and cappuccinos. It’s rich and intense, perfect for a quick pick-me-up!.

Cappuccino

A cappuccino is a coffee drink made with a shot of espresso and equal parts steamed milk and milk foam. It’s rich and bold with a creamy, frothy top. It’s perfect if you love a strong coffee flavor balanced with smooth, airy milk.

Latte

A latte is a coffee drink made with a shot of espresso and lots of steamed milk, topped with a bit of foam. It’s creamy and smooth, with a gentle coffee flavor, making it a popular choice for a cozy, milky treat.
